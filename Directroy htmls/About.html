This website serves as a personal web development portfolio and learning platform. It is designed to showcase my understanding of HTML fundamentals and the structure of modern websites. Through this platform, I aim to demonstrate not only my ability to create clean, semantic HTML but also my attention to layout, accessibility and usability principles that are essential for building user-friendly web experiences. Each page and feature has been crafted with the intention of exploring different HTML elements, linking strategies and content organization techniques, allowing me to experiment and reinforce my knowledge. Additionally, this website acts as a living record of my progress in web development, documenting the challenges I encounter and the solutions I implement. By navigating through the portfolio, visitors can observe how I structure content logically, use headings and paragraphs effectively and link pages in a cohesive manner, all while maintaining a visually coherent and functional design. Ultimately, this project represents a combination of learning, creativity and practical application, serving both as a personal reference and as a showcase for potential opportunities in web development.
